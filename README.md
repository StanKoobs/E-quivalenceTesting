# Code for the paper "Equivalence testing with data-dependent and post-hoc equivalence margins"

---

The paper is available at: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.16213](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.16213)

---

## Reproducing the figures

### Figure 1

Figure 1 is generated with two scripts:
- Left panel (equivalence curve): `LogEquivCurveIntro.R`
- Right panel (e-value curve): `EvalueCurveIntroduction.R`

---

### Figure 2

Figure 2 can be replicated by running:
- `EquivalenceCurvesZandTtest.R`

---

### Figure 3

Figure 3 (geometric illustration of STP\_2 and STP\_3) can be replicated by running:
- `PlotSTP2STP3.R`

---

### Figure 4

Figure 4 can be replicated by running:
- `SequentialSymmetricTtest.R`

---

### Figure 5

Figure 5 can be replicated by running:
- `CompareNumVsTOSTSequential.R`

---

## Notes

- Scripts are intended to be run independently.
